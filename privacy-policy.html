Sibliny Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

Sibliny is a family care coordination tool. We are NOT a healthcare provider, medical device, or HIPAA-covered entity.

1. Information We Collect

Account info (email, name), care data (tasks, appointments, logs), health-related info (medications, check-ins), camera (photos for care logs), and anonymous analytics.

2. Use

To provide care coordination, sync data, send reminders, and improve the app.

3. Protection

TLS encryption, encrypted at rest, row-level security, secure token storage.

4. We Never

Sell data, share with insurers/employers/brokers, use health info for ads, or send health data to analytics.

5. Sharing

Only with your care circle members and service providers (Supabase, Sentry, PostHog, RevenueCat).

6. Roles

Admin: full access. Editor: add/edit. Viewer: read-only.

7. Deletion

Request anytime. Data deleted within 30 days, backups within 90 days.

8. Your Rights

Access, correct, delete, export. GDPR and CCPA rights apply where relevant. We do not sell personal information.

9. Children

Not for under 16. We do not knowingly collect their data.

10. Breach

Per FTC rules, we notify users and the FTC within 60 days.

11. Changes

We notify you of material changes via the app or email.

12. Contact
